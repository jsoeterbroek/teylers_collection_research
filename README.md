# teylers_collection_research

Deep research with AI model claude-opus-4-6 on some 35 random objects from Teylers online collection objects, with specific instructions to compare it's findings with the Teylers metadata of the collection object. 

Goal is to show that AI could be a helpfull tool to augment and re-evaluate some of the knowledge of the object in the collection.

All 35 reports and summary reports are available in the analysis/ directory.
