# teylers_collection_research

Deep research with AI model claude-opus-4-6 on some 50 random objects from Teylers online collection objects plus the 50 objects designated 'Topstukken', with specific instructions to compare it's findings with the Teylers metadata of the collection object. 

Goal is to show that AI could be a helpfull tool to augment and re-evaluate some of the knowledge of the object in the collection.

Summary reports are available in Markdown and PDF, in English and Dutch in the summary folder/.
All individual reports are available in the analysis/ folder.
