# Teylers Museum collection metadata research with AI claude-opus-4-6


Deep research with AI model claude-opus-4-6 on some 50 random objects from Teylers online collection objects plus the 50 objects designated 'Topstukken', with specific instructions to compare it's findings with the Teylers metadata of the collection object. 

Goal is to show that AI could be a helpfull tool to augment and re-evaluate some of the knowledge of the objects in the online collection of The Netherlands' oldest museum.

Go directly to [latest summary report](https://github.com/jsoeterbroek/teylers_collection_research/blob/main/summary/summary-report-20260307.pdf).

All summary reports are available in Markdown and PDF in the summary folder/.
All detailed analysis on individual objects are available in the analysis/ folder.
